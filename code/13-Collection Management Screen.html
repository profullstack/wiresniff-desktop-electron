Help & Tips

Organizing Requests

Use folders to group related requests together. You can drag and drop requests between folders to reorganize your collection structure.

Drag & Drop

Click and hold the grip icon next to any folder or request to reorder them. This helps maintain a logical structure for your API workflows.

Collection Metadata

Add descriptions and tags to make your collections more discoverable and easier to understand for team members.

Tags help filter and search collections across your workspace.

Import & Export

Export your collections as JSON to share with team members or back up your work. Import from Postman, OpenAPI, or cURL to quickly migrate existing APIs.
